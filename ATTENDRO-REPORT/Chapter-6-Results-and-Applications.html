6.1 RESULTS

The developed system "Attendro" was tested under various conditions to evaluate its performance, reliability, and speed. The testing phase yielded the following observations:

  • Biometric Accuracy: The R307 sensor demonstrated a False Acceptance Rate (FAR) of less than 0.001% and a False Rejection Rate (FRR) of approximately 1%.
  • Latency: The average time taken from placing a finger to database update was measured at 1.2 seconds under stable Wi-Fi conditions.
  • Battery Life: The device operated continuously for 8 hours on a single charge of the 2000mAh Li-ion battery.
  • Data Integrity: No data packets were lost during the stress test of 50 consecutive check-ins.

6.2 APPLICATIONS

While primarily designed for educational institutions, the modular nature of Attendro makes it suitable for various sectors:

  1. Corporate Offices: For tracking employee entry and exit times for payroll calculation.
  2. Events and Conferences: To manage guest access to restricted areas securely.
  3. Library Management: Integrating with library databases to track student visits.
  4. Gyms and Clubs: Membership verification without the need for physical ID cards.
